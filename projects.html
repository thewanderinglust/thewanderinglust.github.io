Ara: History Untold

Ara is an evolution in 4X strategy gaming that features an open-ended scoring system, simultaneous player turns, and a living world powered by Oxide Games' exclusive Nitrous engine.

Play as a diverse cast of historical leaders from the fields of military, science, culture, and more!

Gamer Girl

A "harassment simulator" in which you're the only girl in an all guy programming class.

With creeps and weirdos around every corner, you better watch your every step like your academic career (and safety) depends on it! Players are forced to 'woman up' and deal with their fearsome classmates.
